BrushStrokes is an interactive web-based drawing app developed by Showrin Rahman, with futuristic hand-tracking features. Using just a webcam and your index finger, you can draw in the air with real-time gesture control powered by MediaPipe Hands. The app also includes dynamic color selection, adjustable brush sizes, an eraser, and a one-click clear button. For accessibility, it supports traditional mouse and touch input as fallback. Whether you're an artist or just experimenting, BrushStrokes offers a fun, intuitive space to create without limits.
